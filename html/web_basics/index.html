The Hyperloop

About

The founder of Tesla Motors and Space X suggested a crazy idea about having form of high speed train in a vacuum, hovering without friction, to achive record transportation speed available to the many.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Speed: Nothing in mainstream transportation comes close. It promises speed of over 700 MPH!
  • Safety: When thinking about the hyperloop it is often compared with trains. Fortunately, a hyperloop can't exactly derail.

Cons

  • Cost: Building a vacuum through the country is not exactly cheap. When compared to the costs of trains, people think twice about building
  • Safety: Strangely enough, there is concern on both sides about how safe a vacuum could be. (e.g. accidents would be more catostrophic

The Journey

This is still far from reality, so in order for a crazy idea like this to work. The following must happen:

  1. The idea must be profitable to gain interest.
  2. Once profitable, it must become popular, well suported, and gain public aproval.
  3. Policy must be revised for technological advance.
  4. States must agree to allowing contruction.
